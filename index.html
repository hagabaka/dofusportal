What is this?

This website displays coordinates of Dofus dimensional portals which players have posted on ImpsVillage forum threads. Select your server to see them.

How accurate and current the portal coordinates are depends on forum users. There may be a slight delay before this page is updated with new forum posts. By using this site, you acknowledge that the site owner and players posting coordinates disclaim all liability in cases of injuries or deaths resulting from improper dimensional teleportation.

If your server is not yet listed, consider starting a forum thread for your server, and then contact Happida to add it here.
